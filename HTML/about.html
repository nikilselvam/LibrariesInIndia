About Libraries for Kids

The Libraries For Kids project is a program that was set up by Sathana Selvam through her high school library club to buy books for children in India.

In 2013, the club was able to fundraise and establish 2 libraries in rural Indian schools. The project was a success. Schools reported that children wanted school to be open on Saturday to read in the library and that their overall academic performance had improved.

After receiving positive feedback from the schools, the club decided to further expand the project in the summer of 2014, establishing four more libraries. These books have given the children a new medium to expand their knowledge.

The Book for Kids in India program would like to continue this effort to help these impoverished children's education and will continue fund raising!
