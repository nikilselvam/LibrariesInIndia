Libraries for Kids

Libraries in India is a project started by the Sathana Selvam and the Prescott High School Library Club.
The project was featured in the Prescott Daily Courier.
Our goal is to spread education to underprivilleged children in rural Indian schools.
In the past two years, we have managed to establish six libraries and hope to continue doing so.
